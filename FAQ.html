FAQs

What is financial literacy?

Financial literacy is the ability to understand and effectively use various financial skills, including personal financial management, budgeting, and investing.


Why is financial literacy important?

Financial literacy helps individuals make informed decisions about their finances, avoid debt, and build wealth over time.

How can I improve my financial literacy?

You can improve your financial literacy by taking courses, reading books and articles, using budgeting tools, and seeking advice from financial professionals.

What are the basics of budgeting?

Budgeting involves tracking your income and expenses, setting financial goals, and creating a plan to manage your money to meet those goals.

What is the difference between saving and investing?

Saving is setting aside money for future use with minimal risk, while investing involves putting money into assets like stocks, bonds, or real estate with the potential for higher returns and higher risk.
