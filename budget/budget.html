Why create a budget?

Having a budget or a plan can help you with your savings goals. A budget is an organizes plan that can help you keep track of how much money you earn, how much you spend, and how much you have saved. It can help clarify between wants and needs.

How to create a budget?

A need is something you cannot live without, like clothes. A want is something you would like to have but can live without, such as a new guitar. A budget has 3 parts, income, expenses and savings. You should spend 50% of your income on needs, 30% on want and 20% on wants.
