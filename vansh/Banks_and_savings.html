What are Banks?
Why do I need to store my money in a bank?

A bank is a business that accepts deposits and makes loans. When your money is in an insured bank, you don’t have to worry about it being lost or stolen; it will be safe until you are ready to use it.

When you put your money in a bank, it is safe. You don’t have to worry about a fire or a flood destroying your money. You also don’t have to worry about misplacing your money or anyone taking it if it is safely kept in the bank.

Goals and Savings

What are goals? Why are they important?

Goals are things you want to achieve or accomplish. They can be short-term, like by the end of the month, or long-term, like by the time you are old enough to drive.

Goals are important because they help you achieve your goals. They can also help you keep track of your goals
